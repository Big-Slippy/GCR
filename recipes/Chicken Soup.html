General Chicken Soup

Hereâ€™s a super basic, adaptable recipe for chicken soup. Use what you have on hand and feel free to adjust the ingredients and amounts to your liking!

Ingredients:

  • The Chicken: 1 whole chicken (or several chicken breasts)
  • Vegetables: Roughly equal weight to the chicken; any vegetables you have (onions, carrots, celery are ideal, but anything works)
  • Aromatics (Optional): Old onions, carrots, celery, or any aromatic vegetables
  • Grains or Noodles (Optional): Dry noodles, rice, or any grains (remember, they expand during cooking)
  • Spices: Salt, pepper, turmeric (for color), and any other spices you like
  • Herbs and Acids (Optional): Fresh herbs (like parsley) for garnish, lemon juice or vinegar for a bit of acidity

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Broth:
    • Place the whole chicken in a large pot along with any giblets.
    • Add old or less-fresh aromatic vegetables like onions, carrots, or celery if available.
    • Cover with water, bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer and cook until the chicken is tender, about 1-2 hours.
  2. Prepare the Vegetables:
    • While the chicken is cooking, chop fresh vegetables into chunks.
    • Make sure that you use some form of onion. This adds a lot of flavor to the soup.
    • Remember that vegetables will shrink as they cook, so cut them slightly larger than your desired final size.
  3. Remove the Chicken and Strain the Broth:
    • Once the chicken is tender, carefully remove it from the pot and set it aside to cool.
    • Strain out any inedible solids from the broth and discard them.
  4. Cook the Vegetables:
    • Add the chopped fresh vegetables to the strained broth along with a couple of pinches of salt.
    • Simmer until the vegetables are tender, about 30-60 minutes. Add more water if needed to keep the vegetables submerged.
  5. Add Grains or Noodles (Highly Recommended):
    • If using dry noodles, rice, or other grains, add them about 30 minutes before the soup is done.
    • Keep in mind that these will expand by 2-3 times.
  6. Shred the Chicken:
    • While the vegetables are cooking, remove the meat from the cooled chicken.
    • Check and remove bones, cartilage, or other bits, then roughly chop or shred the chicken meat.
    • Add the shredded chicken back into the pot.
  7. Season to Taste:
    • Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning with more salt, pepper, or spices as needed.
    • For a fresh flavor, add a splash of lemon juice or vinegar and stir in fresh herbs if desired.
  8. Serve:
    • Serve the soup hot and enjoy your customizable, hearty chicken soup!
